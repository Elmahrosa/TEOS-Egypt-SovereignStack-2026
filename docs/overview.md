# Overview

TEOS is designed as Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI) for governments,
regulators, and development partners.

The framework enables trusted digital systems without compromising sovereignty,
compliance, or public accountability.
# TEOS Overview

TEOS is a sovereign Web3 implementation stack designed as **Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)**.
It supports institutional deployment of:
- identity and credentials
- governance and decision traceability
- regulated financial records and reporting
- asset registries and infrastructure transparency
- audit-ready compliance and oversight

TEOS is documented for **public-sector readiness** and **development partner review**.
